IEDC RIET

The Innovation and Entrepreneurship Cell (IEDC) of Rajadhani Institute of Engineering and Technology is an actively operating student cell that aims to stimulate and nurture entrepreneurship skills among the students. IEDC was launched on the 16th of March 2016, inaugurated by Dr Biju Ramesh. It exposes the students to a variety of opportunities and peer relationships inside and outside the campus and also works in close association with Kerala Startup Mission. It functions as a bridge between innovative ideas and entrepreneurial ventures by providing technical and non-technical assistance to every student.


Our Vision

To create an innovation culture among Innovators by introducing them the State-of-the-art technologies and positioning the Institution as a Learning and Innovation Platform by delivering technically competent and skilled Entrepreneurs.

Our Vision

To create IEDC as an Innovation Platform and to create future founders by promoting Innovation, Technology and Business Learning among student community
